"How much do I need to study?" or "Where do I stand?". These are the two questions that student ask themselves throghout the education. This is an attempt to help students find the answers to those questions.
E-Perforamance analyzer will let the student feed in their details, provide account-handling capability and let students decide an achievable target for themselves. Based on their entered data EPA plots Graphs indicating a good approximation of the effort they need to put to achieve their target. It also provides a leaderboard which let the student compare his/her course with his/her peers.
