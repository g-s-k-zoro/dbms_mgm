About

"How much do I need to study?" or "Where do I stand?", these are the two questions that haunt every dedicated student throghout his/her education. This is an attempt to help find the answers to these questions.
Edsgar-Perforamance Analyzer lets the student feed in his/her details, provide account-handling capability and helps the student decide an achievable target for him/herself. Based on the entered data, EPA plots Graphs representing a close approximation of the effort the student needs to put to achieve his/her target. The EPA also has a leaderboard which lets the student compare his/her score with his/her peers, promoting a healthy competetion. We also provide Testimonials from successfull pass-outs for guidance, inspiration. Pedigree information about the placement statistics are also available on our portal.
